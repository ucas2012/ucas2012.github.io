Research

Research Summary and Future Plan

  1. Major Research Contributions

    - During my doctoral studies, I focused on analyzing the LIS labor market using NLP(natural language processing) techniques, pioneering research on alternative academic careers in the global LIS field. I also conducted research on scholarly communication and open government data. The library academic evaluation project I led was widely reported in mainland China.

    - During my master's degree, I focused on semantic retrieval of institutional repositories using linked data and developed a name disambiguation algorithm. I was also among the first scholars in China's library field to explore Data Citation standards.

  2. Future Work Plans

    - Teach core courses in Knowledge Organization, Information Behavior, and Research Data Management.

    - Establish a Taiwan LIS workforce database and regularly release labor market reports, making the data openly available to encourage secondary research.

    - Build a scholarly communication platform to integrate important academic information in the field and disseminate it to practitioners through new media platforms.

    - Ensure the production of at least one academic paper per year.

    - Assist in recruiting students from mainland China across all levels in the future.
